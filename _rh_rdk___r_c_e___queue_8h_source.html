12 /* Notes on double-sided material support.
13 
14  When using customized display attributes that specify a custom back-face material, the CRhRdkMaterial that you will retrieve from
15  MaterialFromId will be an internal display material with the type ID exported as uuidRealtimeDisplayMaterialType. To support double sided materials
16  you will need to query this material for its two children, both of which will also be derived from CRhRdkMaterial. The two child materials will be
17  in the child slots named "front" and "back".
18 
19  To support only single sided materials (should your renderer not support double sided materials), simply call "FindChild("front")" on the display material.
20  Otherwise, use FindChild("front") and FindChild("back") to retrieve both.
21 */
